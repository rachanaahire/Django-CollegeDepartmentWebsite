ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science & Information Technology was established in the year 1999. Ruia College was among the first 8 colleges to start B.Sc. Computer Science as part of the curriculum at the Under-graduate level. Later Department introduced M.Sc. Computer Science and M.Sc. Information Technology in the year 2002 and 2012 respectively. Our Department has progressed a lot in so many years along with well-equipped laboratories. We have separate computer laboratories for UG and PG. Every year, leading companies like L & T InfoTech, TCS, Wipro, Capgemini, Infosys, MBT, ATOS, Deloitte India and many more visit the Department for the Campus Placement Drive. Many of our Graduate and Postgraduate students are placed in the above mentioned companies that make us feel proud.
